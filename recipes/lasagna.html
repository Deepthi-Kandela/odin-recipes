Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

It is baked until golden and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles.
  2. Cook the beef with sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  4. Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes.
